Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Pancetta 150g
Eggs 4
Parmesan Cheese 50g
Black Pepper To taste

Step 1: Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.

Step 2: Cook the pancetta in a skillet until crispy.

Step 3: Whisk the eggs and mix with grated Parmesan cheese and black pepper.

Step 4: Combine the drained spaghetti with the pancetta, then mix in the egg and cheese mixture off the heat to create a creamy sauce.

Step 5: Serve immediately with extra Parmesan and pepper.

Classic Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredient Quantity
All-purpose flour 1 cup
Sugar 2 tbsp
Baking powder 2 tsp
Milk 3/4 cup
Egg 1
Butter (melted) 2 tbsp

Step 1: Mix the dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder) in a bowl.

Step 2: Whisk the wet ingredients (milk, egg, melted butter) in a separate bowl.

Step 3: Combine the wet and dry ingredients, stirring until just combined.

Step 4: Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and pour batter to form pancakes.

Step 5: Flip when bubbles form and cook until golden brown. Serve warm with toppings of your choice.

Caesar Salad

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredient Quantity
Romaine Lettuce 1 large head
Croutons 1 cup
Parmesan Cheese 1/2 cup, grated
Caesar Dressing 1/2 cup
Lemon Juice 1 tbsp

Step 1: Wash and chop the romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces.

Step 2: In a large bowl, combine the lettuce, croutons, and grated Parmesan cheese.

Step 3: Drizzle the Caesar dressing and lemon juice over the salad.

Step 4: Toss thoroughly to ensure all ingredients are well coated.

Step 5: Serve immediately, optionally garnished with additional Parmesan.
