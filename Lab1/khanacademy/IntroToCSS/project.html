Travel to Japan

Introduction to Japan

Japan is an island country in East Asia. Located in the Pacific Ocean off the northeast coast of the Asian mainland, it is bordered on the west by the Sea of Japan and extends from the Sea of Okhotsk in the north to the East China Sea in the south.

Popular Destinations

  • Tokyo: A hub for modern culture, technology, and tradition.
  • Kyoto: Famous for its temples, gardens, and traditional wooden houses.
  • Osaka: Known for its vibrant nightlife and street food.
  • Hokkaido: Offers stunning natural landscapes and winter sports.

What to Do

Here are some must-do activities:

  1. Climb Mount Fuji for breathtaking views.
  2. Explore traditional tea houses and learn about Japanese tea ceremonies.
  3. Visit historic castles such as Himeji Castle.
  4. Experience the cherry blossoms during spring.

What Not to Do

Avoid these common mistakes:

  • Talking loudly in public transportation.
  • Sticking chopsticks upright in a bowl of rice.
  • Failing to take off your shoes when entering someone's home.
  • Not learning basic Japanese phrases like "arigato" (thank you).
